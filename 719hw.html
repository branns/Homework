How to make a PB&J

  1. First, gather your ingridients; 2 slices of bread, peanut butter and jelly.
  2. Second, spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. On the other slice of bread spread desired amount of jelly.
  4. Last, combine your slices and enjoy.
